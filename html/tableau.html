This graph shows the profits of the company accross all 50 states. Some states are highly successful, while others are less so. California is the most profitable, at over $76,000. However, eastern states in general have performed better.

California profits are depicted over the past two years by month. Sales in this state have lead to steadily increasing profits, which have gone from about $50 to nearly $4,000. Performance is highly dependent on the time of year.

Californa's increasing sales and profits seem to be particularly focused in one category: office supplies. They have brought in the greatest overall gain for the state of close to $40,000.

Below is depicted the sales of office supplies in California over the past few years. These sales are forecasted to continue to hold steady, slow growth through November of 2015. The forecast shows a slight increase in sales from the previous year, ending in November of 2015 with around $5,000.
